About Me

Hi! Thank you so much for taking the time to look at my portfolio. Let me begin by introducing myself... My name is Aleena Alexia Johns, and I was born and raised in Tacoma, Washington. Electronics have always piqued my interest, as they have for most other children of my generation. In my life, I've seen many incredible digital projects/websites on the daily, and never realized how much time and effort went into each one. Creating my own projects has given me a whole new perspective on the internet and the creators behind it all. To say I am interested in web development is an understatement, and I hope this portfolio gives you a taste of what I am capable of!
